Cox’s Bazar, town, southeastern Bangladesh. It is situated along the Bay of Bengal about 60 miles (100 km) south of Chittagong.

  • Cox's Bazar is one of the longest sea beaches in the world. It’s a sandy beach with an unbroken 155 Km length.
  • The sea beach is the main attraction of the Cox's Bazar town.
  • The municipality covers an area of 6.85 km2 (2.64 sq mi) with 27 mahallas and 9 wards and as of 2012 had a population of 51,918

It’s a wonderful beach to visit for the family, friends and tourists and to enjoy the amazing nature.
